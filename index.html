About Us — Endurance Ride Pakistan

At Endurance Ride Pakistan, we bring together riders and adventure seekers from across the country to experience the thrill of long-distance horse riding. Our mission is to promote endurance riding, train new riders through our Horse Riding School, and explore Pakistan’s breathtaking landscapes on horseback.

From the rugged trails of Chapursan (2021) to the vast plains of Deosai (2022), the golden sands of Nurpur Thal (2023), and the stunning valleys of Chitral Booni (2024 & 2025) — every ride is a journey of courage, skill, and passion.

Join us to ride, train, and endure — where every hoofbeat tells a story.
